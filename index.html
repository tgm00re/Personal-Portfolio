About Me

My interest in computer science started when I attended an introduction to game design course in the seventh grade. This sparked my interest in general programming and led me to improve my knowledge in the field of computer science through self-taught lessons, high school courses, and personal projects. My curiosity regarding computer science has only grown over time and continues to do so.

After nearly 5 years of studying computer science and related fields, I decided to join the Coding Dojo bootcamp to improve my programming skills, learn new technologies, work with a diverse group of individuals, and help me determine what work in software engineering best suits my skills, interests, and personality.
